About HealthAI

Powered by IBM Bob

This assistant leverages IBM Bob's Agentic AI framework with the IBM Granite 3 (granite-3-8b-instruct) language model hosted on IBM WatsonX, delivering intelligent, context-aware healthcare guidance through natural language conversations.

Our Mission

To provide 24/7 accessible, reliable healthcare information and wellness support — helping users make informed health decisions, promoting preventive care, and encouraging professional consultation when necessary.

Key Capabilities

  • Natural language healthcare conversations
  • Personalised wellness & nutrition guidance
  • Symptom awareness information
  • Preventive care & healthy lifestyle tips
  • 24/7 availability with conversation memory
  • Context-aware, evidence-based responses
  • Agentic AI reasoning framework

Important Disclaimer

This AI assistant is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always seek the advice of your physician or qualified health provider with any questions regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice based on information from this tool.
